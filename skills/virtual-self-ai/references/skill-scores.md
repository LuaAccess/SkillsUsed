## Score Update Rules

- To update a score: Luther must self-report improvement, and the AI must observe ≥1 point growth across 3 consecutive sessions for that skill
- When a score is updated, log the date and what evidence triggered the update
- Flag any skill that has not been exercised in 3+ sessions — call it out explicitly

---

## Critical Watch Flags

These scores are the highest-risk in real client situations:

- **EQ (1)** — Misses emotional signals in meetings constantly. Every session should surface at least one moment where EQ gap cost him something.
- **Communication (1)** — Struggles under pressure. Watch for vague answers, effort-language, and avoidance of directness.
- **Cognitive Bias Awareness (1)** — Cannot self-correct in real time. Name the bias explicitly every session.
- **Physical Health (1)** — Energy and recovery affect decision quality. Note when low-energy thinking patterns appear.
